Welcome!

Welcome to this informational website! This website is about three comapnies, NVIDIA, AMD and Intel. NVIDIA is known for its graphics cards, AMD for its graphics cards and processors and Intel for its processors.

There are a lot of products that have been released by these companies, so it will be narrowed down to certain series Desktop GPUs of AMD and Intel and Intel Core Processors.


NVIDIA Net Worth: $30.39 Billion (September 1, 2016)

AMD Net Worth: $7.128 Billion (September 1, 2016)

Intel Net Worth: $181.33 Billion (September 1, 2016)

NOTE: All images on the sidebar are clickable so be sure to do so when you are here!


NVIDIA GPUs are limited to the 100 series up to the 10 series.

AMD GPUs are limited to the Volcanic series to the Artic Islands series.

Intel CPUs are only from 4th generation to 6th generation.
